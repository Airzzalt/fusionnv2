Fusion Network Server Rules and Mod Policy

To ensure fair play and maintain server stability, we have guidelines on which Minecraft mods are allowed or discouraged on our server. Please review the following information carefully.

Prohibited Mods

X-Ray Mods

Mods that allow players to see through blocks or locate specific resources are strictly prohibited.

Elytra Chestplate Swapper

Mods that automate equipment swapping, particularly for Elytra and Chestplates, are forbidden.

Tweakaroo

The Tweakaroo mod and its features are not permitted on our server.

Mouse Tweaks

Mods that enhance mouse functionality beyond vanilla capabilities are not allowed.

Accurate Block Placement

Mods that provide enhanced accuracy for block placement beyond vanilla mechanics are prohibited.

All Crystal Optimizers (except Marlows)

Crystal optimization mods are prohibited, with the exception of Marlows' optimizer.

Freecam

Mods that allow players to detach their camera from their character are prohibited.

Auto Totem

Mods that automatically equip totems of undying are prohibited.

Macros

Any form of macro or automated key pressing/clicking is strictly forbidden.

Inventory Tweaks (with automation)

Inventory management mods that provide automation features are prohibited.

Auto Clicker

Mods or software that automates clicking or actions are not allowed.

Hack Clients (e.g., hitbox, killaura, crystal aura)

All hack clients or mods that provide unfair combat advantages are strictly prohibited.

Fast Clicks

Mods that automate or speed up clicking beyond normal limits are prohibited.

Minimap (with advanced features)

Minimaps with features such as entity radar or underground cave system views are prohibited.

Allowed Mods

All FPS Boosting Mods

Any mod that enhances FPS and optimizes performance is allowed.

Marlows' Crystal Optimizer

Marlows' specific crystal optimization mod is allowed.

Prohibited Clients

Hack/Cheat Clients

All hack or cheat clients, including but not limited to Wurst Client, Impact, Aristois, Sigma, LiquidBounce, and Inertia, are strictly prohibited.

Penalties for Mod Policy Violations

Warning: Zero Tolerance Policy

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for the use of prohibited mods, clients, or any form of cheating. Violations of these rules will result in immediate and severe penalties, including but not limited to:

  • Immediate temporary 30-day ban from the server
  • Blacklisting from all associated Fusion Network services
  • Public listing on our banned players list
  • Disqualification from staff applications

We employ advanced detection methods and actively monitor for mod policy violations to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for all players. There will be no warnings for first-time offenders - use of any prohibited mod or client will result in an immediate ban.

If you're unsure about a specific mod, please ask a staff member before using it on the server.

If you also think that the Disallowed mods are not really considered cheating, make a ticket on Discord.
